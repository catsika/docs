## How Login Works

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to <a href="https://app.keymint.dev" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">app.keymint.dev</a>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the <b>Customer</b> tab on the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter the <b>Vendor Code</b> and your <b>Email</b>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Check the box labeled <b>“First time login / Need password”</b>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click <b>Send Password</b>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    A password will be emailed to the provided address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging In

Once you’ve received your password:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Uncheck the <b>“First time login / Need password”</b> box.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter your <b>Vendor Code</b>, <b>Email</b>, and the <b>Password</b> from your email.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click <b>Login</b> to access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

- Make sure the email matches the one registered by your vendor.
- Passwords are case-sensitive.
- If you don't receive an email, check your spam folder.

<Callout type="tip">
Need help? Contact your vendor or email support@keymint.dev.
</Callout>
