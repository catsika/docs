## Login Process

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Regular Login">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Portal">
        Go to [app.keymint.dev](https://app.keymint.dev) and click the **Customer** tab.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Credentials">
        Enter your **Vendor Code**, **Email**, and **Password**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Access Dashboard">
        Click **Login** to access your license dashboard.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="First-Time Login">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Portal">
        Go to [app.keymint.dev](https://app.keymint.dev) and click the **Customer** tab.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Request Password">
        Check **"First time login / Need password"** and enter your **Vendor Code** and **Email**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Email">
        Click **Send Password** and check your email for the temporary password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Login">
        Return to the portal, uncheck the first-time box, and enter your credentials.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Login Issues" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    - Verify email matches vendor registration
    - Check passwords are case-sensitive
    - Ensure vendor code is correct
    - Try clearing browser cache
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Issues" icon="envelope">
    - Check spam/junk folders
    - Verify email address spelling
    - Contact vendor for email verification
    - Try alternative email if available
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Security Features

<Info>
Your customer portal is protected by industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections, secure authentication, and access logging.
</Info>

- **Secure HTTPS** connection for all communications
- **Email-based authentication** for account verification
- **Session management** with automatic timeout
- **Access logging** for security monitoring

<Warning>
Never share your login credentials with others. If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact support immediately.
</Warning>
